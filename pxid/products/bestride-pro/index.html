About This Product

The BESTRIDE PRO is the professional-grade evolution of the BESTRIDE platform, built for sharing fleet operators and premium consumer brands. It features integrated GPS tracking, reinforced frame construction for heavy-duty use, extended battery range, and an advanced IoT module with fleet management API. Swappable battery support, tamper-resistant hardware, and IPX5 water resistance make it the go-to ODM platform for shared mobility deployments worldwide.

Key Features

01

Purpose-built for sharing fleets: swappable battery, integrated GPS tracking, and 4G IoT module with remote diagnostics and OTA updates.

02

IPX5 water resistance and tamper-resistant hardware ensure reliable operation in all weather conditions and high-vandalism environments.

03

REST API for fleet operators with real-time telemetry: location, battery level, ride status, maintenance alerts, and geofencing.
